HassConsult: Pioneering Real Estate in Kenya

About HassConsult Real Estate

HassConsult Ltd is one of Kenya's most established and influential real estate firms, with roots going back to its founding in 1992. Based in ABC Place, Waiyaki Way, Nairobi, it has helped shape the country's property market through a mix of data-driven research, strategic advisory services, sales and letting, and property development.

At its core, HassConsult combines market intelligence, advisory expertise, and development execution to help homeowners, investors, developers, and financial institutions make informed decisions about Kenyan real estate. The company is well known for publishing the Hass Property Index, a quarterly research tool widely used to track price trends and investment performance across the housing market.

What HassConsult Does

HassConsult's services span the full real estate spectrum:

  • Property sales & letting  Matching buyers and tenants with residential and commercial listings.
  • Valuations  Providing professional valuations for all property types.
  • Property & facilities management  Handling rental collection, maintenance, and operations.
  • Development advisory & consultancy  Using data and market research to guide project design and feasibility.
  • Joint ventures & finance structuring  Partnering with landowners and investors on development deals.

With over three decades of experience and more than 10,000 units completed, the company has built a reputation for credibility and transparency in a sector that often struggles with both.

Notable Projects and Developments

While HassConsult is principally a real estate advisory and agency, it also drives or partners on several property development projects in Kenya:

  • Enaki Township  A visionary experience-themed community in Nairobi featuring life-cycle homes, expansive sensory botanical gardens, leisure facilities, and lifestyle amenities designed around nature and resident experience.
  • Riverside Square  A mixed-use development on Riverside Drive, combining apartments, offices, shops, and leisure spaces tailored to Nairobi's urban lifestyle.
  • Lakehills  A gated community off Kiambu Road with four-bedroom villas and landscaped recreational spaces, aimed at modern family living.
  • Other developments  Properties like Hibo Manor, Lumiere (Brookside), Aura Peponi, Coco (Brookside) and more show the firm's footprint across prime Nairobi and coastal locations.

These projects reflect HassConsult's focus on combining market demand with quality design and long-term value, whether for owner-occupiers or investors.
